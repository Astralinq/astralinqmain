Frequently Asked Questions

What internet speeds do you offer?

We offer a range of speeds from 100 Mbps up to 1 Gigabit (1000 Mbps) to suit different needs and budgets. Our fiber-optic network ensures consistent speeds even during peak usage times.

Is there a contract required?

We offer both contract and no-contract options. Our no-contract plans provide flexibility, while contract plans offer better pricing and additional benefits like free installation.

How long does installation take?

Standard installation typically takes 2-4 hours. Our technicians will schedule a convenient time and ensure everything is working perfectly before they leave.

Do you offer business internet plans?

Yes, we have specialized business plans with dedicated bandwidth, static IP addresses, and priority support. Contact our business team for custom solutions.

What if I experience technical issues?

Our 24/7 technical support team is always available to help. You can reach us by phone, chat, or email. Most issues can be resolved remotely within minutes.
